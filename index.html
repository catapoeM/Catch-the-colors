Hello to the "Catch the colors" game!

1. The goal of this game is to catch as many colors as you can.

2. Be careful, though! Yellow, green and blue give you points,

but the red one takes from you 40 points!

3. You only have 60 seconds to make the best score!

Good luck and Start the game when you feel ready!
